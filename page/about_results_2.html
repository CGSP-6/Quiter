Our Findings

Our findings showed that by using commute-based method, the level of noise pollution for walking routine can be measured and effective. Other than that, this project proves that people can reduce the noise pollution exposure by include green spaces in their walking routine as the noise level in green spaces is much lower than the outside.

The results from this project demonstrates that if pedestrians choose the routes in green spaces to reach their destination, they will be less exposed to noise. Although, the paths which include green spaces takes longer time and route but the option will give different impact and play aremarkable role in reducing the exposure of noise pollution. This can reduce the negative health impact such as hypertension and stress.
